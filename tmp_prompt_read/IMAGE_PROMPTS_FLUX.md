# Prompty do wygenerowania zdjęcia — FLUX / DALL-E / Midjourney

## Prompt główny

Realistic documentary-style photo for a premium Polish dog behaviorist contact page. A calm dog behaviorist is having a gentle conversation with a dog owner in a bright natural home consultation setting. A relaxed medium-sized dog sits or lies near the owner. Warm daylight, soft beige interior, subtle wooden textures, plants in the background, calm emotional atmosphere, professional but human, not staged, not stock-photo looking. The scene communicates trust, empathy, safety and behavioral support. Natural colors: warm off-white, sand beige, muted olive green, soft brown. Premium editorial website photography, clean composition, space for website layout, 4:3 aspect ratio.

## Prompt bardziej „kontaktowy”

Authentic lifestyle photo for a dog behaviorist contact form section. A quiet consultation moment: person with notebook listening to a dog owner, dog calmly nearby, no pressure, no training commands, no veterinary table. Soft daylight through window, minimal Polish home interior, warm neutral palette, calm and reassuring mood. Real people, natural posture, documentary photography, premium service website, subtle depth of field, 4:3.

## Prompt do wersji poziomej

Wide horizontal documentary photo for a contact page hero card. Calm dog owner sitting with dog in a warm bright room while a behaviorist talks gently and takes notes. Relaxed dog, peaceful body language, natural daylight, elegant beige and olive tones, premium minimalist website aesthetic, realistic, non-stock, emotional safety, trust and professional support, 16:10 aspect ratio.

## Negative prompt

generic stock photo, fake smile, golden retriever cliché, vet clinic, dog training obedience, harsh correction, leash pulling, aggressive dog, screaming owner, dominance training, military training, cartoon, illustration, childish paw icons, ecommerce look, overexposed, dark dramatic mood, cluttered background, cheap template, unrealistic hands, distorted dog, extra limbs, text on image, logo, watermark.
